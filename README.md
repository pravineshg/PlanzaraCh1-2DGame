PLANZARA — The Timeline Beyond Time

Overview :

PLANZARA is a 2D story-driven RPG inspired by classic Pokémon GBA games.
The game combines science fiction, time travel, exploration, and mystery in a futuristic world.

Story :

The story begins in the year 2187 in Valora City. Three students—Suzene, Mits, and Reza Silvera—prepare 
for a school time-travel project at Ad-itya Sam Rocks Academy.

During an experiment, a space-time distortion occurs and they are accidentally 
transported to the year 2026 instead of the Dinosaur Era
. After several failed attempts to return home, they discover that they are trapped inside a mysterious locked timeline.

As they search for answers, they learn about the legendary organization PLANZARA, its leader Captain Shanks
, and the mysterious Captain Opra, who is believed to be connected to the timeline's isolation.

The first chapter ends with a mysterious flashback involving Dr. Aizen Silvera,
leaving the true identity of Captain Opra as a cliffhanger.

Main Characters :

Suzene – Main playable character

Mits – Technology enthusiast and trader

Reza Silvera – Intelligent student interested in history

Captain Shanks – Leader of PLANZARA

Captain Opra – Main antagonist

Dr. Aizen Silvera – Scientist connected to the mystery


Features :

Pokémon GBA-inspired gameplay

Top-down 2D exploration

Story and dialogue system

Futuristic city environments

Time-travel themed missions

Interactive NPCs

Cinematic cutscenes


Technologies Used :
